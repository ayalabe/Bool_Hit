Game Instructions – Breakdown:
At the beginning of the game the player chooses four colors
From the color table,
And he must know that there are no colors that repeat themselves.

The selected colors will appear to the player on the screen
And at the end of the four selections the player,
Will see the colors hidden where he had an injury and where was Bull.

Each turn has to play 5 possibilities to guess by hints that give it the colors in their exact location.
